About This Project

Dynamic Tune - One Place for All Your Music Needs

This comprehensive AI-powered music platform combines cutting-edge machine learning with creative AI tools to provide a complete music experience. Whether you want to discover new songs, create original music, or generate lyrics, we've got you covered!

Intelligent Recommendations: Using TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) and cosine similarity algorithms, we analyze song lyrics, genres, and musical features to find songs that perfectly match your taste.

Weather Integration: Our standout feature detects your location, analyzes current weather conditions, and suggests music that complements the mood of the moment.

Creative AI Tools: Integrated with Suno AI for music generation and built-in AI lyrics creation, making this your one-stop destination for all music-related needs.

Technologies Used:

Python Flask Bootstrap 5 Scikit-learn TF-IDF OpenWeatherMap API Suno AI AI Lyrics Generator HTML5/CSS3 JavaScript
